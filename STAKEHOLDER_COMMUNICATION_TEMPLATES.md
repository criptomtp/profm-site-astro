## Conference Call Script (30 Minutes)

**Opening (2 min):**
"Thanks for making time. I've been analyzing our 36 fulfillment guides and identified a significant opportunity to drive organic growth. This is a focused 9-week campaign that should deliver +2,500 monthly sessions and 6-80x ROI. I'll walk through the strategy, then open it up for questions."

**Opportunity (5 min):**
"We have 36 guides already written—high-quality content on fulfillment, 3PL, shipping, operations. The issue is visibility. Right now they're getting <500 monthly organic sessions. If we build backlinks strategically and optimize technical foundation, these guides can drive thousands of sessions. The market demand is proven—people search for this stuff constantly."

**Strategy (8 min):**
"Three-track approach:

**Track 1 - Foundation (Week 4):** We optimize the site technically. Internal linking (connecting guides to each other), schema markup for search engines, Core Web Vitals. This ensures when backlinks come in, they actually translate to rankings. Without this, backlinks are 30% less effective.

**Track 2 - Link Building (Weeks 5-12):** We execute targeted outreach to 50+ websites. Four batches:
- Batch 1: High-authority industry blogs (2-5 links expected)
- Batch 2: Authority business/marketing sites (3-5 links)
- Batch 3: Software/community platforms (3-5 links)
- Batch 4: Unlinked mention recovery (5-10 links)

All personalized pitches, high quality. No spam, no paid links.

**Track 3 - AI Visibility (Parallel):** Syndication to Medium, Dev.to, Reddit, Quora. Builds brand awareness and drives parallel traffic while SEO builds.

All tracked weekly with a KPI dashboard so we can optimize in real-time."

**Investment (3 min):**
"$10,000-15,000 covers:
- Tools (link research, ranking tracking): $900-1,800

## FAQ Responses (For Email or Chat)

**Q: How much will this actually cost?**
A: $10,000-15,000 covers all tools and contractor labor. If you have internal team available, cost is just $900-1,800 in tools. If you need to hire, figure $15,000-20,000 total.

**Q: What's the actual ROI?**
A: Conservative scenario: +1,500 sessions/month by Month 3 = $22,500/month revenue (assuming $15/session value). Payback in 1-2 months, then 6x+ annual ROI. Optimistic: +3,500 sessions/month = $70,000/month revenue, 12+ month ROI.

**Q: Why should we do this instead of paid ads?**
A: Paid ads are rent (you pay forever). Organic SEO is own (once you have rankings, traffic keeps coming). This campaign builds a 12-month compounding asset. Plus, we have existing content—no writing cost.

**Q: What if competitors do this too?**
A: The backlinks we acquire are permanent (even if competitor gets more links, ours stay). Plus, early movers win—we get first pick of outreach targets. First-mover advantage = 2-3 month head start.

**Q: How do we measure success?**
A: Weekly KPI dashboard (backlinks acquired, organic sessions, top guides, engagement rates). Monthly detailed analysis. Go/No-Go decision at Week 8: Are we on pace for 35+ backlinks and +2,500 sessions? If yes, continue. If no, evaluate adjustments.

**Q: What if we don't get stakeholder approval this week?**
A: Campaign timeline shifts. Week 4 becomes Week X (whenever approved). All documentation remains valid. The guides aren't going anywhere. We can restart anytime, but earlier is better (seasonal advantage).

**Q: Who runs this?**
A: You need three roles: Outreach Coordinator (send emails, manage responses), Technical Lead (implement Week 4 optimization), Analytics Owner (track metrics weekly). Can be in-house, contractor, or mix. I have complete onboarding packages for each role.

**Q: How much time will it take?**
A: Week 4: 11-18 hours one week (technical lead only). Weeks 5-12: 40-50 hours/week for outreach coordinator, 5-10 hours/week for analyst, 10-15 hours/week for SEO oversight. Total: 100-150 hours over 9 weeks.

**Q: What if it doesn't work?**
A: We've built contingency plans for 8 major failure modes. Low response rate? Switch to guest posts. Slow traffic? Accelerate batches and optimize for featured snippets. CWV issues? Optimization checklist ready. Link quality concerns? Disavowal process documented. No contingency is campaign-killer.
