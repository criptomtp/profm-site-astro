### Could Competitors Block Our Growth?

**Risk 1: Competitor Launches Similar Campaign**
- Medium probability (they could copy our strategy)
- Mitigation: We move faster (Week 4 start vs. their delay)
- First-mover advantage: We secure Batch 1-2 targets before they notice

**Risk 2: Competitor Buys Our Target Domains**
- Low probability (most targets are blogs, not for sale)
- Mitigation: Our targets are content-based, not company-based

**Risk 3: Algorithm Penalizes "Newer" Link Profile**
- Very low probability (Google favors steady growth over time)
- Our growth rate (~5-6 links/week) is natural, not spammy

**Risk 4: Competitors Increase Their Link-Building**
- Medium probability (they may accelerate)
- Mitigation: Our content quality differentiates us (harder to copy)
- Our focused outreach strategy is more efficient

**Overall competitive risk: LOW**. Market is large enough for 5+ players. We don't need to "beat" competitors; we need to build authority in our segment.
