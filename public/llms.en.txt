# MTP Group Fulfillment — English version

> 3PL fulfillment operator in Ukraine. Two warehouses (3,900 m² total) in the Kyiv region, 150+ active e-commerce clients, 60,000+ shipments per month. Operating since 2014. Director-authored content by Mykola Liashchuk (founder).

Key facts: 3,900 m² Category B+ warehouse (Shchaslyve 2,800 m² + Bilohorodka 1,100 m²), 150+ active clients, 60,000+ monthly shipments, integrations with Nova Poshta / Ukrposhta / DHL, OTIF 99.8%, backup power (3 generators + Starlink) — we operate during nationwide blackouts, service languages: Ukrainian / Russian / English, pricing from 18 UAH/order + 650 UAH/m³/month storage.
