Export Logistics

Ukraine represents a strategic geographic arbitrage opportunity for international publishing. Our proximity to the EU land borders and established air-freight corridors from Warsaw and Kyiv allow for 2-5 day global delivery at costs significantly lower than domestic 3PLs in the West.

We handle all customs declarations (CN22/CN23) and commercial invoices required for duty-free thresholds in major markets like the US (Section 321) and the EU (IOSS).

Seasonality Management

The publishing industry experiences extreme volatility during Q4 and major book fairs (Frankfurt/London). Our facility is engineered for elasticity; we scale from 1,000 to 10,000 daily shipments without degrading accuracy metrics.

Our staffing model utilizes a highly trained industrial reserve team specifically dedicated to manual ISBN cross-referencing during peak surges, ensuring your pre-orders ship on time, every time.
