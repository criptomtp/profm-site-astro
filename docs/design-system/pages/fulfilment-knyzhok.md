## Archetype decision — DIFFERENT per language (intentional)

This is a deliberate departure from "1 archetype per cluster" pattern. Rationale: 3 audiences are fundamentally different and visual language must match.

### 🇺🇦 UA — DIRECT mood

- **Audience:** Ukrainian publishers + book retailers + self-published authors
- **Buyer behaviour:** wants quick contact form, action-oriented, Cyrillic warmth, Nova Poshta + Rozetka reality
- **Hero:** full-width with overlay (book stacks photo), badge "ФУЛФІЛМЕНТ ДЛЯ ВИДАВНИЦТВ", H1, prominent hero CTA form
- **Reference:** `/fulfilment-dlya-odyahu/` (Direct mood working pillar)
- **WOW-element:** **"ISBN Live Counter"** — visible widget showing "X тиражів книг проходить через MTP цього місяця" (manually updated stat that signals scale)

### 🇷🇺 RU — EDITORIAL mood

- **Audience:** CIS publishers (KZ/MD/GE) entering Ukrainian book market + Russian-language Ukraine publishers
- **Buyer behaviour:** appreciates seriousness, typographic resonance with books category, longer reading patience, formality
- **Hero:** pure typography (no image), DM Serif Display H1, drop-cap in lede
- **Reference:** Editorial archetype (similar to en/faq style but for content pillar)
- **WOW-element:** **"Ledger Pricing Table"** — styled as accounting ledger with sharp 1px black borders, no shadows (matches Editorial system already generated by Stitch)

### 🇬🇧 EN — INDUSTRIAL mood

- **Audience:** International publishers + indie self-pub authors selling cross-border + brands using Ukraine as low-cost logistics base
- **Buyer behaviour:** data-driven, ROI-focused, expects metrics + cost comparisons + war-resilience proof
- **Hero:** split (text left, dark hero image right), uppercase tracked-out label "BOOKS & PUBLISHING / UKRAINE 2026", H1, stats bar immediately below
- **Reference:** `/en/fulfilment-for-clothing/` (Industrial archetype)
- **WOW-element:** **"Cost Comparison Table US/EU vs Ukraine"** — explicit per-order cost matrix: $0.41 (UA) vs $1.50 (US 3PL avg) vs $2.20 (EU 3PL avg)
