## 1-page TL;DR — what we tell a Moldovan brand today

If a Moldovan seller calls and asks "can MTP fulfill our orders for Ukrainian buyers from a Kyiv warehouse?" — yes, here is the rough script:

1. **Inbound (Moldova → Ukraine warehouse):**
   - Two reliable channels: **Nova Post B2B cargo** (door-to-door up to 1,000 kg per shipment, 3-day transit) for small inbound batches, or **road groupage / FTL** through carriers like Hellmann, Meest Cargo, KLG, AsstrA for pallets.
   - Border crossing of choice: **Otaci ↔ Mohyliv-Podilskyi** (Northern, the busiest cargo corridor, ~24/7), with **Criva ↔ Mamaliga** as backup. **Mayaki–Udobne–Palanca** (Southern, near Odesa) was hit by a Russian drone strike on the Dniester bridge on **18 December 2025** — still degraded, only one-way Ukraine→Moldova passage in places, do not plan freight on this corridor in 2026.
   - Documents needed: **CMR consignment note** + **commercial invoice** + **packing list** + **EUR.1 movement certificate** (if MD-origin goods, gives 0% UA import duty under the bilateral FTA — Protocol 2021, in force 24 May 2023). For longer transits or transit through 3rd countries — TIR carnet.
   - Ukraine import duty under bilateral UA-MD FTA: **0% on most goods** with EUR.1 origin proof. Without origin proof — Ukraine MFN rate applies (mostly 0–10% for cosmetics/fashion/non-food).
   - Ukrainian import VAT 20% applies on customs value + duty (cannot be avoided, but Moldovan brand registers as importer or works through MTP as commission agent).

2. **Outbound (Ukraine warehouse → Moldovan buyer):**
   - Default carrier: **Nova Post UA → Nova Post MD** branch-to-branch. **From 330 UAH starting** for a parcel up to ~120 cm length. Transit **3 days from a Ukrainian branch to a Moldovan branch**. Up to 30 kg parcel / up to 1,000 kg cargo / max dims 300×170×170 cm.
   - Alternative: **Meest Express** (especially for parcels >30 kg or Ukrainian diaspora customers); **Ukrposhta** (cheapest, slow, postal).
   - **COD supported on Nova Post UA→MD**; settlement to Ukrainian seller next business day via NovaPay. Recipient pays in MDL/card/bank transfer in Moldova; seller receives UAH equivalent. Free insurance up to 500 MDL.
   - **Customs declaration:** sender + recipient names must be **Latin script**. CN23 + commercial invoice (in UA + EN) for shipments over 150 EUR. Under 150 EUR per shipment → exempt from MD import duty/VAT until **30 September 2026** (see #2 below — this changes from October 2026).

3. **Critical 2026 changes the team must know:**
   - **From 1 October 2026 (Moldova):** the 150 EUR de minimis exemption goes away in current form. Parcels up to 150 EUR will be subject to **standard 20% VAT**. This destroys the unit economics of single-parcel UA→MD low-AOV e-commerce. Either: (a) Moldovan brand pre-clears bulk into MD warehouse, or (b) keeps stock with us in UA and we ship UA→MD with VAT included in price. Plan now.
   - **From 1 July 2026 (EU):** EU itself removes its 150 EUR exemption, replaces with flat 3 EUR/parcel customs duty + VAT. Affects MTP's EU-bound flow, not direct UA-MD, but worth tracking.

4. **When a UA-warehouse model makes sense for a Moldovan brand:**
   - Approx. threshold: **>200–300 orders/month into UA** OR a Moldovan brand that already imports from EU/CN suppliers and would prefer one consolidation hub close to the UA market (40M+ population vs MD's 2.6M). Below 200/month — probably cheaper for them to ship from MD direct via Nova Post.
   - Strong fit: cosmetics, fashion, accessories, supplements, low-volume premium FMCG with UA distribution ambitions.

5. **Volume fact about Nova Post in Moldova:** ~19+ branded offices in cities (Chișinău, Bălți, Cahul, Edineț, Căușeni, Orhei, Fălești etc.) and **~250–300 parcel lockers** as of late 2025 — solid last-mile coverage for a 2.6M-person country. Source: Nova Post MD official + AIN report.

## 3. Moldovan customs

### De minimis (current, valid until 30 September 2026)
- **Individuals (personal use):** parcels with intrinsic value ≤ **150 EUR** are exempt from import duty AND VAT. [Trade.gov Moldova guide](https://www.trade.gov/country-commercial-guides/moldova-import-tariffs), [Moldova MFA customs rules](https://mfa.gov.md/en/content/customs-rules)
- **Legal entities (businesses):** threshold drops to **100 EUR** for VAT exemption. Above that — full customs procedure.
- Exclusions from de minimis (always pay tax): **alcohol, tobacco, perfume, eau de toilette**, and any "non-occasional" / commercial-pattern goods.

### Critical change: 1 October 2026
- The 150 EUR de minimis exemption for parcels is **abolished in its current form** — parcels worth ≤150 EUR will be **subject to the standard 20% VAT**. [Pravda Moldova report](https://md.news-pravda.com/en/world/2026/05/05/97111.html), [Earlier Dec 2025 report](https://md.news-pravda.com/en/world/2025/12/16/84152.html)
- Customs duty exemption status above 150 EUR — already required H6 declaration since Aug 2024.
- **Operational implication for MTP:** any Moldovan e-commerce business plan based on cheap individual UA→MD parcels must factor in 20% VAT from October 2026 onwards. Moldovan recipient pays it via MPay; alternatively, seller pre-pays via DDP arrangement.

### VAT and duty calculation (commercial parcels)
- **Standard MD VAT: 20%** on (customs value + duty + transport cost).
- **Customs duty:** ad valorem, ranges **0–25%** depending on HS code. [WTO tariff profile MD](https://www.wto.org/english/res_e/statis_e/daily_update_e/tariff_profiles/MD_E.PDF)
- **For UA-origin goods:** the **Moldova–Ukraine bilateral FTA** (signed 2003, amending Protocol 2021 in force 24 May 2023) gives **preferential 0% duty** on most goods if accompanied by a **Ukrainian EUR.1 movement certificate** (issued free by UA customs). [e-broker.md FTA explainer](https://www.e-broker.md/en/useful-information/reguli-de-origine-preferentiala-diversificate-in-comertul-liber-dintre-republica-moldova-si-ucraina)
- Without EUR.1 → MFN rate applies. For most fashion/cosmetic items, MD MFN sits at 5–15%.

### Required customs documents (commercial parcel >150 EUR)
- Commercial invoice with: sender + recipient legal details, HS code per line item, country of origin, unit price, total value, currency, Incoterm.
- Packing list.
- CN23 (postal) or CMR (road) consignment.
- **EUR.1 movement certificate** if UA-origin, to claim 0% duty.
- For parcels 150.01–1,000 EUR: **H6 electronic declaration** in **ASYCUDA World** via https://ecustoms.trade.gov.md/individual-person
- For parcels >1,000 EUR: full customs declaration via **broker** (Nova Post MD offers it; or third-party at e-broker.md).

### Who fills out the customs docs
- **Postal items via Nova Post / Meest:** carrier pre-fills CN23 from the sender's waybill data. For commercial >150 EUR, **the Moldovan recipient** logs into ASYCUDA personal portal OR delegates to broker.
- **MTP role:** we should be able to pre-prepare a **commercial invoice template + EUR.1 application** for the seller to streamline; we can NOT submit ASYCUDA H6 on behalf of the MD recipient (recipient/broker only). Rec: partner with one Moldovan customs broker so we hand off complex shipments cleanly.
- Reference: [logos-pres.md "parcels moved online"](https://logos-pres.md/en/news/parcels-have-been-moved-online/), [moldovalive.md new rules](https://moldovalive.md/new-rules-for-declaring-international-mail-items-over-150-euros/)

### Official Moldova customs URL
- Customs Service of Moldova: **https://customs.gov.md/**
- E-customs portal: **https://ecustoms.trade.gov.md/**
- E-customs FAQ (Romanian): **https://customs.gov.md/ecustoms/intrebari-frecvente.html**

- **Currency conversion:** MDL collected from buyer → seller receives **UAH** in Ukrainian bank account or NovaPay wallet. Conversion rate: **NovaPay's internal rate** at settlement date — confirm specific rate sheet via Nova Post UA business team. As of 5 May 2026, mid-market is **1 MDL ≈ 2.54 UAH**. - Not supported on UPU-route postal parcels (UA→MD).

- **Per-COD fee:** historically 2% of cash value with a minimum (Ukrainian domestic NP charges ~20 UAH min); for international UA→MD COD, request rate card from Nova Post for Business: **https://novapost.com/en-md/for-business/pay-on-delivery/**

### Documents required MD → UA (B2B import to Ukraine)
- **CMR consignment note** (3 copies, sender + carrier + recipient). [IRU CMR template](https://www.iru.org/sites/default/files/2017-01/how-to-fill-cmr-model-iru-2007-en.pdf)
- **Commercial invoice** with HS codes, value, Incoterm.
- **Packing list.**
- **Certificate of origin** — for MD-origin goods, request **EUR.1 movement certificate** from Moldovan customs at export; this allows **0% UA import duty** under the bilateral FTA (Protocol 2021 in force May 2023).
- **TIR carnet** — only if transit through 3rd countries (e.g. MD→Romania→Hungary→UA), not needed for direct MD→UA road. Both are signatories to TIR Convention.
- **Sanitary/veterinary/phyto certificates** — for food, supplements, cosmetics with active ingredients. Required by UA Derzhprodspozhyvsluzhba.
- **EU CE / certificates of conformity** — for electronics, toys, etc., if applicable.

### Ukrainian customs clearance time
- **Standard road import: 1–3 days at the border + 1–2 days at internal customs (if 2-stage clearance).** Most freight forwarders quote total UA customs window of 2–5 days for clean documentation.
- **EUR.1 origin proof** speeds it up and reduces duty exposure to 0%.

## 6. Ukrainian customs for incoming Moldovan goods

### Ukraine-Moldova FTA — what applies
- **Bilateral FTA** since 13 November 2003, **Protocol 2021 amending it** in force from **24 May 2023**. [trade.gov.md / e-broker.md confirmation](https://www.e-broker.md/en/useful-information/reguli-de-origine-preferentiala-diversificate-in-comertul-liber-dintre-republica-moldova-si-ucraina)
- Both sides also part of **Pan-Euro-Mediterranean (PEM) Convention** (Moldova since 1 Sep 2015, Ukraine since 1 Feb 2018) — allows **diagonal cumulation of origin** with EU/Turkey/EFTA suppliers.
- Result: most goods originating in Moldova enter Ukraine at **0% import duty** with EUR.1.
- **Sensitive products** (small list): Ukraine has historically excluded a few agri products from full liberalisation (e.g. some sugar, ethyl alcohol). For e-commerce / fashion / cosmetics / supplements / FMCG packaged goods → **0% duty applies**.

### Standard import duties (without preference, MFN — for reference)
- **Cosmetics, perfume:** 5–10% MFN.
- **Apparel, textiles:** 0–10% MFN.
- **Footwear:** 8–13% MFN.
- **Food (packaged, non-sensitive):** 0–10% MFN.
- **Electronics:** mostly 0% MFN (Ukraine ITA member).

### Ukrainian import VAT (always applies, no exemption from FTA)
- **20% VAT** on (customs value + import duty + delivery cost up to UA customs).
- VAT is reclaimable for VAT-registered Ukrainian importers (incl. MTP if acting as importer-of-record on commission terms).

### When does FTA NOT apply
- **Goods produced in 3rd country, imported through MD** without sufficient transformation in MD → no MD origin → no preference. Example: a Moldovan brand reselling Italian apparel — that apparel needs **separate EU/Italian EUR.1** to enter UA at preferential rate (PEM cumulation can help if origin chain is properly documented). Otherwise, **MFN duty applies**.
- **Certificates required for EU producers' goods reselled by MD brand:** original EU EUR.1 (issued by EU exporter's customs) or **REX statement** from EU exporter. PEM diagonal cumulation needs **proper supplier declaration chain** — talk to a customs broker.

### Customs clearance time UA-side
- **2–5 days typical** for clean docs at internal customs (Kyiv-region terminals).
- **Same-day at border** for declared transit; +1–2 days at internal customs for full clearance.

## 7. Operational reality from a Moldovan seller perspective

### Why would a Moldovan brand keep stock in Ukraine?
- **Ukrainian market = 40M+ population vs MD's 2.6M.** UA is 15× the addressable consumer base. A MD brand growing UA orders past 50–100/month rapidly hits the wall on direct-from-MD shipping economics (parcel cost dominates AOV).
- **Single FX cycle:** keep stock UA-side, accept UAH from buyers, repatriate margin once a month — saves on per-parcel FX losses on COD.
- **Faster delivery:** UA→UA 1–2 days vs MD→UA 3–5 days = better customer experience, lower return rate.
- **Returns processing in UA:** MTP processes returns at half the per-shipment fee (variable 9–13 UAH per item depending on tier; 50% rule confirmed 2026-05-04).
- **Ad spend ROI:** UA Google/Meta ads convert better when delivery promises are 1–2 day, not 5.

### Volume threshold where UA-warehouse model becomes economical
- **Rule of thumb: ~200–300 orders/month into Ukraine.**
- Below: direct UA→MD parcels via Nova Post are simpler (no UA warehousing fee, no inbound trucking).
- 200–500/month: UA warehouse breaks even or slightly wins on unit economics.
- 500+/month: strongly favors UA warehouse model.

### Seasonality
- **Peaks:** late November (Black Friday), pre-Christmas (mid-Dec), pre-Easter (March/April), September (back-to-school), May (Mother's Day, MD national holidays). [Moldova ecommerce report 2025](https://www.amcham.md/st_files/2025/11/25/eCommerce%20Market%20in%20Moldova%20in%202025.pdf)
- **Cross-border-specific peak:** UA diaspora gift season Dec–Jan (Ukrainians abroad sending to UA family, but reverse also exists).
- Border congestion peaks: late Dec / pre-major Orthodox holidays. Plan inbound stocking 2–3 weeks earlier.

### Currency considerations
- **MDL (Moldovan leu):** local-market pricing in Moldova. 1 MDL ≈ 2.54 UAH (May 2026).
- **UAH:** all UA storage/fulfillment fees, all ad spend, all UA-domestic shipping costs.
- **EUR:** standard for B2B inbound trucking invoices, customs broker fees, and most cross-border contracts.
- **MTP recommendation:** quote MD client in EUR for clarity; settle in EUR or MDL via SWIFT monthly. Internal cost basis stays in UAH.

### Ukrainian e-commerce market context (for sales pitch)
- UA e-com expected to reach **~$15–18B GMV in 2026** (vs MD's **$1.06B 2025 / projected $1.67B 2030**).
- 40M-population vs 2.6M = 15× demand pool.
- Major UA marketplaces a Moldovan brand should know: **Rozetka, Prom.ua, OLX, Allo, Epicentr.** For independent stores: **Shopify, WooCommerce, Hostpro/Tilda** common.

## 9. Real-world risks specific to UA↔MD route

### Border closure / war scenarios
- **Maiaky–Udobne / Palanca corridor (Southern, Odesa-Chișinău M-15 highway via Dniester bridge):** **STRUCK by Russian drone 18 December 2025.** As of late Dec 2025 / early 2026, only one-way Ukraine→Moldova passage at points; freight rerouted via Northern crossings. **Operational rule for 2026: do NOT plan freight or parcels through Mayaki–Udobne. Use Otaci–Mohyliv-Podilskyi as default.** [Ukrainska Pravda, 18 Dec 2025](https://www.pravda.com.ua/eng/news/2025/12/18/8012489/), [NV.ua](https://english.nv.ua/nation/ukraine-moldova-agree-alternative-routes-after-russian-strike-on-dniester-bridge-50571578.html)
- **Transnistria region (Russia-controlled left-bank MD):** **Full blockade in effect.** Ukraine + Moldova jointly enforce closure since 2022 (UA-side closed Transnistria border) and tightened further in March 2026 (MD refused phytoproducts/veterinary drugs to Tiraspol). Multi-layered fortification on Ukrainian Odesa-region border since spring 2026. **Implication for MTP: any UA→MD parcel addressed to Transnistria addresses (Tiraspol, Bender/Tighina, Rîbnița, Dubăsari) is HIGH-RISK — likely undeliverable / returned. Reject these orders or route only via Chișinău address with onward MD-internal delivery handled by buyer.**
- **Russian missile/drone strikes on Odesa region infrastructure:** ongoing. Plan inbound routing through Vinnytsia (Otaci) corridor, not Odesa corridor.

### Sanctions & legal edge cases
- **EU sanctions list:** check recipient (especially business buyers) against EU sanctions map: https://www.sanctionsmap.eu/. Some MD-registered businesses operate in Russian-language commercial sphere with possible Russian beneficial ownership — flag for legal review on B2B shipments >5,000 EUR.
- **UA sanctions on MD individuals/entities:** small list, mostly Transnistria-linked. Check NSDC sanctions registry before B2B contract.
- **MD-EU Association Agreement** does NOT extend sanctions automatically; MD applies its own list, but generally aligns.

### Currency restriction edge cases
- **Ukrainian National Bank (NBU) wartime restrictions:** still in force May 2026. Outbound currency transfers from UA require justification (invoice, contract). For MTP receiving MDL/EUR from MD client: inbound has no NBU friction; outbound (e.g. paying MD broker fee in EUR) needs invoice-supported SWIFT.
- **Cash limits at MD border:** crossing personal cash >10,000 EUR equivalent requires declaration on both sides. Not relevant for parcel ops, only personal travel — but tell management not to carry >10k in cash on site visits.

### Operational fraud / chargeback risk
- COD via Nova Post UA→MD: **buyer can refuse parcel** at MD branch (typical refusal rate 5–10% in cross-border; higher for low-trust categories). Refused parcels return to UA — MTP pays return shipping (50% of original rate).
- Mitigation: **partial prepayment** (e.g. 100 MDL via card upfront) for higher-AOV orders; **whitelist Moldovan postal codes** with track record.

## Operations playbook — quick decision tree

```
Inbound query (MD brand wants UA fulfillment):
  → Volume <100 orders/month UA?
      → Recommend they ship MD→UA direct via Nova Post; revisit at higher volume.
  → Volume 100-300/month?
      → Pilot: 1 pallet inbound via Nova Post B2B cargo (1,000 kg max), 30-day stock test.
  → Volume >300/month?
      → Full UA fulfillment offer: groupage inbound monthly + UA storage + UA last-mile via Nova Post UA + COD if applicable.

Outbound query (UA client shipping to MD buyer):
  → AOV <50 EUR, weight <2 kg, no rush?
      → Ukrposhta (cheapest, slow). 8-21 days.
  → AOV 50-150 EUR, weight <30 kg, COD wanted?
      → Nova Post UA→MD branch-to-branch + COD via NovaPay. 3-5 days.
  → AOV >150 EUR or B2B?
      → Nova Post UA→MD with full commercial invoice + EUR.1 + flag MD recipient to file H6 declaration (or arrange broker).
  → AOV >1,000 EUR or urgent?
      → DHL Express door-to-door + dedicated customs handling. 2 days.

Risk flags:
  → Recipient address in Tiraspol / Bender / Rîbnița / Dubăsari?
      → REJECT order. Transnistria, undeliverable.
  → Routing via Mayaki-Udobne / M-15 highway through Palanca?
      → Reroute to Otaci-Mohyliv-Podilskyi via Vinnytsia. M-15 still degraded post Dec 2025 strike.
  → Order date is Sept 2026 or later, AOV <150 EUR?
      → Brief customer / seller: 20% MD VAT now applies (1 Oct 2026 rule change), price accordingly.
```
