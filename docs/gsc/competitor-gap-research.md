## 2. Three Pillar-Topics Where Competitors Dominate and MTP is Absent/Weak

### Pillar A: "Що таке фулфілмент" — Educational / Top-of-Funnel Content
Every major competitor has a long-form explainer blog post that ranks broadly: diffreight ("простими словами"), senderukraine ("переваги, недоліки, кому підійде"), flg.one ("гайд від оператора"), horoshop, shop-express, logiclink, osa.net.ua, olx.ua business, keepincrm. These pages harvest "what is fulfillment" impressions across dozens of long-tail variants. MTP has only transactional /services pages — no pillar educational hub ranking for informational queries.

### Pillar B: "Порівняння / ТОП операторів" — Listicles & Comparison Content
SERP is dominated by third-party listicles (dropplatforma "ТОП 12", cpashka "ТОП 11", keepincrm, keyCRM "кого обрати"). MTP is rarely mentioned. These articles drive comparison-stage buyer traffic. MTP has no "MTP vs Nova Poshta vs Meest" or "Як обрати фулфілмент-оператора" comparison content to rank in this cluster and capture outbound clicks.

### Pillar C: Marketplace-Integration Pages — Rozetka / Prom / Epicentr / OLX / Kasta
Rozetka launched its own fulfillment in 2021 (Forbes.ua coverage). Competitors like unipost, zammler, 4erdak, ptl-group explicitly market "фулфілмент для Rozetka", "інтеграція з Prom.ua", "OLX доставка". MTP /services lists vertical pages (22 `fulfillment-dlya-*`) but has NO marketplace-integration pages — which is where transactional buyer intent concentrates in 2026 UA market.

## 5. Top Recommendations — What to Build First

### Recommendation 1: Pillar educational hub `/ua/shcho-take-fulfilment/` (+ RU, EN)
Long-form 3,000–4,000-word guide: "Що таке фулфілмент, як працює, скільки коштує, кому підходить, як обрати оператора". Target Pillar A + queries #3, #8. Internal-link hub that cross-links all 22 vertical pages and /calculator. This is the single highest-impression opportunity (1,000–2,000 vol/mo).

### Recommendation 2: Dedicated `/pricing/` (тарифи) page — NOT calculator
Competitors rank on `senderukraine.com/prices`, `unipost.ua/calculate-2`, `flf.com.ua`. Build transparent price table: storage (грн/м³/місяць), збирання замовлення (грн/шт), пакування, повернення, прийомка, додатковий товар в замовленні. Include comparison bullets vs. Nova Poshta / PTL / Meest pricing. Targets query #2 (300–600 vol/mo) plus long-tail "скільки коштує фулфілмент". MTP has the cheapest tier on the market ("від 18 грн") — surface it as a price-anchor page, not just in calculator.

### Recommendation 3: Marketplace-integration landing pages
Three new pages: `/ua/fulfillment-dlya-rozetka/`, `/ua/fulfillment-dlya-prom-ua/`, `/ua/fulfillment-dlya-olx/` (+RU, EN). Each covers: API integration, label printing, NP+UP delivery from our warehouse, returns handling. Kasta/Epicentr as v2. Targets query #7 (150–300 each = 450–900 total), plus catches comparison-intent visitors leaving Rozetka's own fulfillment.

### Bonus (Tier 2): `/ua/vidpovidalne-zberigannia-kyiv/` — semantic adjacency page
500–1,000 vol/mo query, dense competition but MTP is entirely absent. Pitch: Boryspil location, climate control, 24/7 power (generators), insurance. Reuses existing warehouse assets — no operational change, just semantic surface area.

---

## Key Insight

MTP's 22 `fulfillment-dlya-*` vertical pages cover **goods-type** semantic axis well, but the market searches predominantly on **4 other axes** where MTP has zero coverage: (1) audience/business-size, (2) marketplace integration, (3) service-type adjacencies (відповідальне зберігання, 3PL, cross-border), (4) pricing/comparison. The fix is 5–8 new pillar/landing pages per language covering these 4 axes — not more vertical pages.
