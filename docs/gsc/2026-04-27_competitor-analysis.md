## 3. Top-3 Competitor Page Audit

**unipost.ua** — full service tree (`/poslugi/skladska-logistika/`, `/kross-doking/`, `/upravlyajte-skladom-onlajn/`, `/calculate/` x2 langs); D2C blog content for top-of-funnel; UA+RU mirrors. Pricing hidden behind calculator. No public customer logos. Strength: breadth of pillar URLs.

**senderukraine.com** — `/en/prices` is a true static price table with all line items in UAH + minimum payment rule + payment FAQ. `/how-it-works` is a dedicated pillar. Blog covers "що таке фулфілмент", CRM systems, top services. **Best transparency play in market.** No logos / no case studies — surprising weakness.

**4erdak.com.ua** — 6 Kyiv warehouse locations exposed in nav, every audience as a separate URL (`fulfilment-dlja-malogo-biznesu`, `fulfilment-dlja-internet-magazinu`, `vidpovidalne-zberigannja`, `sezonne-zberigannja-shin`, `kvartirnij-pereizd`). UA+RU mirrors. Headline price anchors ("від 19 грн", "від 350 грн/м²"). 5/5 rating widget. FAQ section present. Strength: audience-axis depth + geo-multiplication.
