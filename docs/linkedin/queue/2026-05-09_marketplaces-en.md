# EN — Ukrainian marketplaces for international sellers

Rozetka. 30M monthly users. GMV bigger than Amazon's entry into Poland.

Most Western brands have never heard of it.

We wrote a primer on the Ukrainian marketplace landscape for international sellers looking at 40M-consumer Ukraine + cross-border to CIS:

→ Rozetka (45% share) — Amazon-like, strict content requirements, aggressive self-promotion
→ Prom.ua (30%) — closer to eBay, lower commissions, easier onboarding for SMB
→ OLX Ukraine — C2C but strong for fashion, electronics second-hand
→ Epicentr, Kasta, Allo — vertical-specific, worth it for niches

What international sellers usually miss:
→ Payment rails — local UAH settlement vs USD/EUR repatriation
→ Returns policy — consumer law is EU-aligned since 2022
→ VAT — 20% standard, but 0% export regime available for certain flows
→ Logistics integration — most require Ukrainian-address warehouse (this is where 3PL enters)

If you're evaluating emerging EU-adjacent markets — Ukraine is the one nobody's pitching you, and that's the opportunity.
