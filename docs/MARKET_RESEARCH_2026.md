# MARKET RESEARCH: MTP Group Fulfillment (fulfillmentmtp.com.ua)
## Comprehensive Analysis of the Ukrainian 3PL/Fulfillment Market
## Date: April 2026

---

## PART 1: COMPETITOR ANALYSIS

### 1.1 Market Landscape Overview

The Ukrainian fulfillment market in 2026 consists of approximately 15-20 active operators ranging from divisions of national logistics giants (Nova Poshta) to specialized boutique 3PL providers. The market is fragmented, with no single operator holding more than 15-20% market share (excluding Nova Poshta's delivery dominance).

### 3.6 Key Market Trends

**Growing:**
1. **FOP explosion**: +62% new FOP registrations in e-commerce (2025) = massive new demand for outsourced logistics
2. **Marketplace dominance**: Rozetka, Prom, Kasta driving sellers toward fulfillment (marketplace fulfillment requirements)
3. **International expansion**: Ukrainian brands selling to EU via Shopify/Amazon — need cross-border fulfillment
4. **Automation adoption**: 74% of companies face labor shortages, driving WMS/automation investment
5. **Average order value growth**: +23% in USD terms (H1 2025) makes per-order fulfillment pricing more attractive

**Declining/Challenging:**
1. **Order count growth slowing**: 2025 growth was only 7% (vs 25% in 2024) — market maturing
2. **War-related risks**: Energy instability, staff mobilization, logistics disruptions
3. **Inflation pressure**: Costs rising across all logistics inputs (fuel, labor, rent)
4. **Payment terms**: COD (cash on delivery) rates declining but still dominant = capital lock risk
