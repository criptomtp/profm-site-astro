## Red Flags to Watch For

❌ **If Coverage stays at 0 after 48 hours:**
- Check GSC for errors
- Verify robots.txt is not blocking pages
- Check for noindex meta tags on pages
- Request re-crawling in GSC

❌ **If QS doesn't improve by Day 3:**
- Check landing page experience score
- Verify mobile responsiveness
- Check page load times
- Review ad copy relevance

❌ **If no organic impressions by Day 5:**
- Verify hreflang tags are correct
- Check canonical tags
- Ensure pages are not noindex
- Submit manually in GSC
